Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks
- Use official apps and websites for transactions
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Regularly check your bank statements for suspicious activity
- Do not install unknown apps from untrusted sources
- Keep your phone and apps updated

UPI Safety Tips:
- Never approve collect requests from unknown people
- Always double-check the receiver’s details before sending money

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
